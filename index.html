Welcome to Our Church

Welcome to Alpha Omega Full Gospel Church, where we believe in the complete gospel message and the transforming power of Jesus Christ. We are a Spirit-filled community dedicated to worship, fellowship, and spreading God's love.

Our church embraces the full gospel message, including salvation, divine healing, baptism of the Holy Spirit, and the second coming of Christ. We are committed to helping every person discover their purpose and grow in their relationship with God.

Whether you're seeking spiritual guidance, looking for a church home, or simply curious about Christianity, we welcome you with open arms.

What We Believe

Salvation

We believe in salvation through faith in Jesus Christ and His atoning work on the cross.

Holy Spirit Baptism

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit as an empowering experience for believers.

Divine Healing

We believe in divine healing through the power and authority of Jesus Christ.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our guide for living.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Second Coming

We believe in the imminent return of Jesus Christ to establish His kingdom.
